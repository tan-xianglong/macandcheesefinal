About Us

We specialize in, you guessed it, Mac & Cheese!

Using our delicious house recipe as a canvas, our team has experimented like mad cheese scientists, combining favorite comfort flavors into a skillet of perfection, each one cradled within the curves of traditional Mac & Cheese. Everything is freshly made in house and all of our Mac & Cheese can be made low carb or gluten free.
